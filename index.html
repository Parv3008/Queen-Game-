Game Rules:

  1. Place the queens on the board such that no two queens attack each other.
  2. Queen cannot be in the same row as another queen.
  3. Queen cannot be in the same column as another queen.
  4. Queen cannot be in the same diagonal as another queen.
  5. Queen must be placed in a different colored region (each region can contain at most one queen).
